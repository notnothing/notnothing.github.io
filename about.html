About

Not Nothing is a small press. We publish unfashionable works for this millennium and the next.

We’re interested in packaging writing that comes out of collectives, scenes, and online forums so that their discourses may outlast them. In the age of Anna’s Archive and The Pile, we believe the book should be lighter and move faster. That means experimenting with how the book is constructed and distributed. We make books for writers and researchers on the move.

San Francisco, New York, São Paulo, Turin.
